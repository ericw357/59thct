59th Ct
Secured Mailbox Proposal

Replace the two aging central mailbox clusters with two Dark Bronze Model 1570-12 secured cluster mailboxes in the same locations. These cluster boxes will be purchased by the neighborhood and maintained by the neighborhood.  The US Postal Service only manages the cluster box; they do not maintain the unit. If the unit becomes damaged in some form or another, the neighborhood is responsible for the shared cost of repair/replacement.


The cluster boxes are pre-configured. This proposal sets up each site to be able to serve your address and allow you to continue to collect your mail at the same site that you have been wile maintaining balanced neighborhood mail traffic. This his means there will be a number of unassigned/unused mailboxes at each location (3 or less).
